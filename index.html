About Us

Nurturing Brilliance
in Rajasthan

Founded in Sotwara, Nawalgarh, Aryawart School has been a beacon of quality education in the Jhunjhunu district. Our philosophy is simple: every child is unique, every child can shine.

With dedicated faculty members, modern infrastructure and a tradition of academic and co-curricular excellence, we are proud to serve the students and families of Rajasthan.
